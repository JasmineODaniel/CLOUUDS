Everything you need to know about Clouuds—Answered.

Can't find what you're looking for? Our team is always happy to help.

Is there a free plan available?
Yes! Our free plan includes up to 3 projects and 5 team members. No credit card required.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
How does the team member limit work?
Team member limits count active users. Guests with limited access do not count toward your limit.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
Yes, we offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact support@clouuds.io to apply.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored on AWS in the US and EU. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant.
